The objectives of the conference are to bring together academicians, researchers, and industry practitioners to exchange and share experience and research results in various aspects of Computer Science and Information Technology. It also provides a platform for discovering new ideas and building networking among researchers, practitioners, and educators and also to provide a platform for researchers and practitioners to share ideas of Computer Science and Information Technology and research experiences.
The College of Engineering Perumon was started during the year 2000 under the Co-operative Academy of Professional Education (CAPE) on the banks of the Astamudi lake, which is included in the tourism map of Kerala. The special ambience is most suited for an ideal place for the establishment of an Engineering College intended to acquire a deemed University status in due course.
The Department of Computer Science & Engineering was established in the year 2000. It offers the best training to develop high-quality, technical, and scientific manpower to meet the challenges. The Department provides a strong emphasis on the fundamentals of computer education and training so that students can grow on the desired career path they choose, namely teaching, research, or industry.

CALL FOR PAPER

The researchers, academicians, professionals, industrialists, and students are invited to submit their original research papers on the following areas:
  • Computer Vision & Image Processing
  • Robotics
  • Cloud & Fog Computing
  • Grid & Edge Computing
  • Distributed Computing
  • Data Analytics
  • Data Mining
  • Blockchain Technologies
  • IOT based systems
  • Machine Learning
  • Deep Learning
  • Cyber Security
  • Network Security
  • E-commerce
  • Any other topics in Computer Science & its Applications

GUIDELINES TO AUTHORS

Authors are invited to submit original and unpublished papers related to conference themes in A4 size IEEE format in .docx format with a maximum of 6 pages to ricit@perumonec.ac.in. The authors per paper are restricted to a maximum of six. All the papers will be peer-reviewed, and the selected papers will be accepted for the conference.
